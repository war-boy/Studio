About Us

We are a small educational services company. We offer courses on 6 different subjects within our field of expertise. For each course subject, we offer a beginner, intermediate, and advanced course. All beginner courses are on Monday and Tuesdays, all intermediate courses are on Wednesday and Thursdays, and all advanced courses are on Fridays and Saturdays. Each course is 2 hours long.
